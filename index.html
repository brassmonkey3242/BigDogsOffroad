Big Dogs is owned by a military veteran

Welcome to Big Dogs Offroad, the Home of the best Four Wheelin’ on the east coast. Located on and partnered with the Cove Campground in Gore,VA Big Dogs Offroad has been organizing large scale trail-rides for over twenty years.

All Big Dog Events have - a food vendor, hot showers 24/7, 26 electrical campsites, a sandy beach, shooting range, toilets and sinks, RV dump station.

All Big Dog events benefit Humane Society, ASPCA, BARCs Animal Rescue Center, and Gore Volunteer Fire DEPT (GVFD)
